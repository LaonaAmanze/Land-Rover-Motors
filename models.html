RANGE ROVER

The Range Rover
A luxury SUV produced by Land Rover, a British automotive brand under Jaguar Land Rover (JLR). First introduced in 1970, it is known for combining off-road capability with high-end luxury. Over the years, the Range Rover has evolved into a premium vehicle with advanced technology, refined interiors, and powerful engine options, including gasoline, diesel, plug-in hybrid, and fully electric models. The latest generation features state-of-the-art infotainment systems, adaptive suspension, and all-wheel drive, making it a top choice for those seeking a mix of performance, comfort, and prestige. It competes with luxury SUVs like the Mercedes-Benz G-Class, BMW X7, and Bentley Bentayga.
